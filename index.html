Features

End-to-End Sales Support

Customer Communication Handling: The platform manages all buyer inquiries, from questions about item specifics to negotiation on price, sparing the seller from back-and-forth communication.

Order Fulfillment and Shipping: For smaller items, the service offers packaging and shipping on behalf of the seller, while also arranging pickup for larger items if purchased locally.

Secure Payments: Payments are processed securely and transferred to the seller after the sale, with a clear and transparent commission structure.

User-Friendly Dashboard for Sellers

Sales Tracking and Notifications: A dashboard shows all active listings, with real-time notifications on views, inquiries, and sales status.

Earnings and Analytics: The platform provides detailed reports, helping sellers track their earnings and see which items or categories perform best.

Re-Listing Options: If items aren’t selling, the platform suggests ways to improve the listing or adjust the price, helping sellers stay proactive.

How it works

In-Home Photography Service

Professional Photography: Trained photographers visit the customer’s home to capture high-quality photos, making listings more attractive to potential buyers.

Staging Assistance: The photographers help stage items in the best light, enhancing visual appeal to improve sale potential.

360° Photos and Video Tours: The service includes 360-degree images and short video tours for items like furniture or appliances, giving buyers a detailed look from home.

Item Assessment and Pricing Guidance

Market Evaluation: The service evaluates each item’s market value and suggests pricing based on current demand and similar listings.

Category Expertise: Specialized knowledge in categories like electronics, antiques, fashion, and more to accurately assess each item’s worth.

Dynamic Pricing: Automated price adjustments can be applied based on buyer interest and time listed, maximizing the chance of sale without constant price management from the seller.

Easy Listing Creation and Management

Automated Listings: After the in-home session, the service generates and uploads listings on the website, including all photos and descriptions.

Customizable Listing Options: Sellers can edit or approve listings directly through the website or app if they want control over certain details like descriptions or keywords.

Cross-Platform Sales Integration: Listings are automatically synced with popular marketplaces, maximizing exposure to potential buyers.
